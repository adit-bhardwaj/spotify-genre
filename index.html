HIP HOP

Hip hop is a genre of music and cultural movement that originated in African American and Latino communities in the Bronx, New York City, in the 1970s. It encompasses various elements, including rap music, DJing, breakdancing, and graffiti art. Hip hop has since become a global phenomenon and has had a significant impact on music, fashion, language, and overall popular culture. know more

R&B/SOUL

R&B, short for Rhythm and Blues, is a genre of popular music that originated in African American communities in the United States in the 1940s. It combines elements of jazz, blues, gospel, and soul music, with a focus on rhythm, groove, and soulful vocals. R&B has played a significant role in shaping the modern music landscape and has influenced numerous genres, including pop, hip hop, and contemporary R&B. know more

EDM

Electronic dance music (EDM), also known as dance music, club music, or simply dance, is a broad range of percussive electronic music genres made largely for nightclubs, raves, and festivals.
